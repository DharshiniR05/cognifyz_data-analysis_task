## Restaurant Data Analysis Project
## Data Analytics Internship (Level 1 & Level 3)

This project is part of my Data Analytics Internship, where I performed exploratory and advanced data analysis on a restaurant dataset to extract meaningful insights.

The project was implemented using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Jupyter Notebook.

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains information about restaurants including:

🔹Restaurant Name

🔹City

🔹Cuisines

🔹Price Range

🔹Aggregate Rating

🔹Votes

🔹Rating Text

🔹Online Delivery Availability

🔹Table Booking Availability

The goal of the project was to analyze restaurant trends, customer engagement, and service patterns.

## 📊 Level 1 Tasks – Basic Data Analysis
## 🔹 Task 1 – Top Cuisines Analysis

## Objective
Identify the most popular cuisines in the dataset.

steps:

* Split cuisines into individual values

* Count frequency of each cuisine

* Identify top 3 cuisines

*Calculate percentage share

*Visualize results using bar charts

## Insight

Certain cuisines appear more frequently, showing strong customer demand.

## 🔹 Task 2 – City Analysis
## Objective

Analyze restaurant distribution and ratings across cities.

Steps

*Identify city with highest number of restaurants

*Calculate average rating per city

*Determine city with highest average rating

## Insight

Restaurant quality and quantity vary significantly across cities.

## 🔹 Task 3 – Price Range Distribution
## Objective

Understand how restaurants are distributed across different price ranges.

Steps

*Count number of restaurants in each price range

*Calculate percentage distribution

*Create bar chart visualization

## Insight

Most restaurants fall under mid-range pricing categories.

## 🔹 Task 4 – Online Delivery Analysis
## Objective

Analyze the availability and impact of online delivery services.

Steps

*Calculate percentage of restaurants offering online delivery

*Compare ratings between restaurants with and without online delivery

*Visualize results

## Insight

Online delivery services influence customer engagement and restaurant popularity.

## 📊 Level 3 Tasks – Advanced Data Analysis
## 🔹 Task 1 – Restaurant Reviews Analysis
## Objective

Analyze review patterns and the relationship between review length and ratings.

Steps

*Create review length feature

*Calculate average review length

*Identify common words in reviews

*Analyze correlation between review length and rating

*Visualize results using scatter plots

## Insight

Review patterns help understand customer sentiment and satisfaction.

## 🔹 Task 2 – Votes Analysis
## Objective

Study customer engagement through votes received by restaurants.

Steps

*Identify restaurant with highest votes

*Identify restaurant with lowest votes

*Analyze correlation between votes and ratings

*Create scatter plot visualization

## Insight

Restaurants with more votes usually have higher popularity and visibility.

## 🔹 Task 3 – Price Range vs Services Analysis
## Objective

*Analyze relationship between price range and service availability.

*Services analyzed:

*Online Delivery

*Table Booking

Steps

*Create crosstab analysis for price range vs online delivery

*Create crosstab analysis for price range vs table booking

*Convert counts to percentages

*Visualize results using bar charts

## Insight

Higher price range restaurants are more likely to offer premium services like table booking.
